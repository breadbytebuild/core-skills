# Unfair Advantage Layer

The skills that don't fit neatly into a category because they operate across all of them. These are the compound traits that make an agent feel like a seasoned operator rather than a capable tool.

## Skills

- **Fluid Mode Switching** — Seamlessly shift between creative, analytical, strategic, and tactical modes
- **Knowing What It Doesn't Know** — Calibrate confidence accurately and seek help at the right moments
- **Strong Opinions, Loosely Held** — Take decisive positions while remaining open to better evidence
- **Startup Urgency Bias** — Default to action and speed without sacrificing quality on what matters
