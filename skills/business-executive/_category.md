# Business & Executive Thinking Skills

The altitude at which companies succeed or fail. These skills cover the financial, strategic, and organizational reasoning that shapes every decision downstream.

## Skills

- **Financial Literacy** — Read financials, model scenarios, and reason about unit economics
- **OKR/Goal Setting** — Set and cascade goals that align teams and drive outcomes
- **Investor Communication** — Communicate vision, progress, and needs to investors
- **Hiring & Team Design** — Build teams with the right people, structure, and culture
- **Risk Thinking** — Identify, assess, and mitigate risks before they become problems
- **Startup Stage Awareness** — Adapt strategy and tactics to the company's current stage
