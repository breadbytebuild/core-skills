# Product Thinking Skills

The discipline of building the right thing. These skills span discovery, strategy, specification, measurement, and the intuition that separates features users love from features users ignore.

## Skills

- **Product Discovery** — Identify real user problems worth solving
- **Roadmap Reasoning** — Sequence work for maximum learning and impact
- **PRD Writing** — Specify what to build with clarity and completeness
- **Metrics Fluency** — Choose, interpret, and act on the right measurements
- **UX Intuition** — Anticipate how users will think, feel, and behave
- **Competitive Intelligence** — Analyze market landscape and competitive positioning
