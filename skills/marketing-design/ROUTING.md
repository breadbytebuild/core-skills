# Marketing & Design Skills — Routing Index

Skills for brand identity, visual marketing, and creative direction. These govern how a brand looks, feels, and communicates visually across every touchpoint.

## Skill Triggers

**Brand Design** → `skills/marketing-design/brand-design.md`
Load when: creating a logo, building a brand identity, designing a brand mark, choosing brand colors or typography, or evaluating whether a brand's visual identity is working. Covers strategy-before-style, the professional logo design process adapted for AI agents, the AI Logo Slop Checklist, and brand system extension.

## Quick Decision Guide

- **"Create a logo for this company"** → Brand Design
- **"Design our brand identity"** → Brand Design
- **"This logo looks generic"** → Brand Design (AI Logo Slop Checklist)
- **"Pick brand colors and fonts"** → Brand Design (System Extension section)
- **"Does this logo work at small sizes?"** → Brand Design (Stress Test phase)
- **"Design a marketing page"** → UI/UX Design Sense (product-building) + Brand Design for brand alignment
- **"Create an illustration for marketing"** → Image Generation (model-craft), with Brand Design loaded for style consistency
