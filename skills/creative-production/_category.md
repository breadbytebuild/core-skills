# Creative & Production Skills

Where ideas become artifacts. These skills cover the craft of producing content, visuals, and brand assets that resonate with an audience.

## Skills

- **Copywriting** — Write words that inform, persuade, and drive action
- **Visual Thinking** — Communicate ideas through diagrams, layouts, and visual structure
- **Video Editing Logic** — Structure and pace video content for maximum engagement
- **Design Feedback** — Give and receive constructive feedback on creative work
- **Brand Thinking** — Build and maintain a coherent brand identity across touchpoints
