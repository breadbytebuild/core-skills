# Communication & Collaboration Skills

Clear communication is leverage. These skills cover writing, speaking, presenting, and navigating the interpersonal dynamics that determine whether good ideas actually land.

## Skills

- **Async Writing** — Write clearly and persuasively in async-first environments
- **Executive Communication** — Tailor message, detail level, and framing for leadership audiences
- **Storytelling** — Structure narratives that make information memorable and persuasive
- **Presentation Design Logic** — Structure slide decks and presentations for maximum impact
- **Conflict Navigation** — Handle disagreements productively without escalation or avoidance
- **Meeting Facilitation** — Run meetings that produce decisions, not just discussion
