# Growth & Marketing Skills

Getting the right product in front of the right people. These skills cover positioning, channels, campaigns, and the growth loops that compound over time.

## Skills

- **Positioning & Messaging** — Define how a product is perceived relative to alternatives
- **Content Strategy** — Plan and produce content that attracts and retains an audience
- **Performance Marketing** — Run and optimize paid acquisition across channels
- **Email Marketing** — Build relationships and drive action through email
- **Community & PLG** — Grow through community engagement and product-led motions
- **Launch Playbooks** — Plan and execute product launches for maximum impact
