# Operational Excellence Skills

The engine room. These skills ensure that great strategy translates into consistent execution through processes, documentation, and systematic improvement.

## Skills

- **Project Management** — Plan, track, and deliver work on time and on scope
- **SOPs & Documentation** — Create documentation that people actually use
- **Debugging Mindset** — Systematically diagnose and resolve problems
- **Feedback Loops** — Design systems that surface and act on feedback
- **Context Switching** — Move between tasks and domains without losing effectiveness
